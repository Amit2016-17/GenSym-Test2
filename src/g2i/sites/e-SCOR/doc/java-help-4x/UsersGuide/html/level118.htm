Configuring the Products a Role Sources and Delivers

Once you have created the product hierarchy and the supply-chain model, you can configure the products a role sources and delivers by assigning product specifications to roles. You assign the delivery product of a Base Manufacturer, Distributor, and Manufacturer role, and you assign the source product of a Consumer role.

e-SCOR creates the appropriate product composites-source or delivery products-and places them in the Products pool on the role's detail.

For example, if you assign to a Manufacturer role a computer, which consists of a computer case and electronics components, e-SCOR creates these product composites in the Products pool on the role detail:
