Topic - Using Pull and Push Planning Modes

Configuring the Amount to Push

If an upstream supplier is pushing a single delivery product to a single downstream buyer, you configure these parameters for these product composites:

Push Amount of the delivery product of the supplier role and of the source product of the buyer role.
Replenishment Threshold and Minimum Reorder Quantity of the delivery product of the supplier role.
Starting Inventory of the delivery product of the supplier role.
Inventory Maximum of the source product of the buyer role.

The supplier delivers the entire Push Amount to the buyer, assuming the Push Amount of the buyer's source product is a number greater than 0. Typically, you configure the Push Amount of the source and delivery products to be the same number. A Push Amount of 0, the default, indicates that a buyer does not want to accept any source products from its upstream supplier.

The Starting Inventory of the supplier's delivery product determines the initial amount to push.

If the downstream buyer specifies an Inventory Maximum for its source product that is less than the Push Amount of the upstream supplier role, the buyer throws away the excess inventory.

The supplier delivers the products as soon as it can, based on inventory levels for source products.

When configuring a role to use push mode, the role's delivery product must not use replenishment planning.

To configure the amount to push:

1 Show the Products pool detail of the supplier role.
2 Display the properties dialog of the delivery product, click the Planning tab, and configure the Push Amount parameter to be the amount to push.
3 On the Planning tab, configure Replenishment Threshold and Minimum Reorder Quantity parameters of each delivery product to be 0.
4 Show the Products pool detail of the upstream supplier role.
5 Display the properties dialog of the delivery product being pushed, click the Inventory tab, and configure the Starting Inventory parameter of the supplier's delivery product.
6 Show the Products pool detail of the downstream buyer role.
7 Display the properties dialog of the source product being pushed, click the Planning tab, and configure the Push Amount parameter to be the same number as the Push Amount of the supplier role's delivery product.
8 Click the Inventory tab and configure the Inventory Maximum parameter of the buyer's source product.
