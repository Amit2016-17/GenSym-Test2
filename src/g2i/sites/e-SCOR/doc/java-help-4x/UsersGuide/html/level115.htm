Creating and Connecting Individual Roles

When a buyer role sources products from a single upstream supplier role and when the supplier role delivers products to a single downstream buyer role, you connect individual roles together.

To create and connect individual roles, you simply drag the stubs of the same color together to form two paths between the two roles.

To create and connect individual roles:

1 Display the e-SCOR toolbar and click the Roles tab.
This toolbar contains the Base Manufacturer, Distributor, Manufacturer, and Consumer roles.
2 Place on the model detail as many roles as are required to describe your supply chain, given your product hierarchy.
Place Base Manufacturer roles on the left side of the workspace and Consumer roles on the right side of the workspace.
3 Configure the Role Label parameter to be unique within the model.
If the model contains only one occurrence of a particular type of role, you do not need to configure the label.
4 Drag the input stub of one role into the output stub of the other role, then click to connect the two stubs to form a path.
5 Move one of the roles slightly to reconfigure the paths to form a straight connection.
Tip: To ensure that the roles are connected, always move one of the roles to reconfigure the paths. If the roles are not connected, the paths do not reconfigure and the simulation will not run as expected.
