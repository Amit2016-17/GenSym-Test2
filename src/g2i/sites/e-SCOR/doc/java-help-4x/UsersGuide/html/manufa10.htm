Topic - Modeling a Manufacturing Process

Level 1 Metrics to Analyze

At the role level, you can analyze the:

Financial metrics that the Manufacturer role computes, based on the Net Selling Price parameter of the upstream supplier's delivery product.
Asset metrics that the role computes, based on inventory levels.
Cost metrics that the role computes, based on costs associated with the various source and delivery management tasks.

For details about these metrics, see Viewing Metrics for Roles.
