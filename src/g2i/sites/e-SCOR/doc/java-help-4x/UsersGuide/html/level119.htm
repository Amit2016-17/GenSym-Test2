Assigning Product Specifications to Roles

When assigning product specifications to roles, follow these guidelines:

Assign each product specification in the product hierarchy to at least one role.
A supplier role can deliver multiple products, and a Consumer role can source multiple products.
Multiple roles can source, make, and deliver the same product.
In general, you assign components to upstream supplier roles and finished products to downstream buyer roles.
In general, you assign products that a role delivers to downstream buyers; the exception is a Consumer role where you assign products that the role sources from upstream suppliers.
Note: We recommend that you finalize the supply chain before you assign products to roles. Changing the product hierarchy requires that you reconfigure the supply chain and reassign products to roles, which can be time-consuming if you have many roles.

You have two choices for assigning product specifications to roles:

Assign the product specification to the role through the role dialog.
Assign the product specification to the role interactively.

The results are the same, regardless of the technique you use.
