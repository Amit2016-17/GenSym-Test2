Verifying the Assignment of Product Specifications

To verify that you have assigned a product specification correctly, you can show the roles associated with a product specification, and vice versa. You can also show product composites associated with product specifications, and vice versa.

To show the roles associated with a product specification:

Choose Show Roles on a product specification.

A magenta arrow appears next to each role associated with the product specification.

To show the product specification associated with a role:

Choose Show Products on a role.

A magenta arrow appears next to each product specification associated with the role.

To show the product composite associated with a product specification:

Choose Show Product Composite on a product specification.

A magenta arrow appears next to each product composite in the Products pool associated with the product specification.

To show the product specification associated with a product composite:

Choose Show Product Specification on a product composite.

A magenta arrow appears next to each product specification associated with the product composite in the Products pool.
