Configuring a Distributor to Deliver its Source Products

Your supply chain might have a Distributor role that delivers the products it receives from its upstream suppliers. These products can be components or finished products.

When you configure a Distributor role to deliver its source products, e-SCOR creates a single product composite in the Products pool for the product specification you assign to the role. This product composite is automatically configure to be both a source product and a delivery product.

The following figure shows a model in which the Distributor role sources and delivers components. When you assign the component to the Distributor role, e-SCOR creates a product composites for the component, which is automatically configured to be both a source product and a delivery product.

The next figure shows a model in which the Distributor role sources and delivers finished products. The Distributor role sources computers from the Manufacturer role, which manufactures them from components. When you assign the finished product to the Distributor role, e-SCOR creates a product composite for the finished product, which is automatically configured to be both a source product and a delivery product.

To configure a Distributor role to deliver its source products:

Assign to a Distributor role a component or finished product.

For details, see Configuring the Products a Role Sources and Delivers.
