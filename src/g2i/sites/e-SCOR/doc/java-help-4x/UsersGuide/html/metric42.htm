Quantity to Deliver

The total number of orders for delivery products that a supplier has received since the start of the simulation.

Quantity Shipped

The total number of delivery products that a supplier has shipped since the start of the simulation.

Metrics

In Transit Amount

The total number of delivery products that a supplier has taken from inventory and is waiting to deliver.
