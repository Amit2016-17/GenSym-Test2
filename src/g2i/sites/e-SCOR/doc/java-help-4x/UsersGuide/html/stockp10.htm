Example: Amount to Order is the Minimum Reorder Quantity

Here is an example where the Amount to Order is the Minimum Reorder Quantity, because the amount needed to maintain desired inventory levels is less than the Minimum Reorder Quantity:

Inventory Level 120
Replenishment Threshold 100
On Order 10
Awaiting Order Total 50
Minimum Reorder Quantity 30

120 - 100 + 10 = 30 (Inventory Level - Replenishment Threshold +
                     On Order)

30 < 50 (30 < Awaiting Order Total)

100 - 120 - 10 + 50 = 20 (Replenishment Threshold - Inventory Level -
                          On Order + Awaiting Order Total)

max [30, 20] ( max [Minimum Reorder Quantity, 20] ) therefore:

Amount to Order = 30
