e-SCOR User's Guide
Topic - Welcome

Audience

The online help is for e-SCOR users who want to understand the concepts and techniques required to create, run, and analyze supply-chain models, using e-SCOR. e-SCOR users are typically consultants or business people, who are familiar with supply-chain modeling and SCOR. Although users are expected to be familiar with SCOR, this guide gives a basic overview of the SCOR modeling concepts that relate to e-SCOR. If you are not familiar with SCOR, visit www.supply-chain.org.
