Navigating to Product Composites

To edit the parameters of the source and delivery products of a role, you must display the detail of the Products pool for the role. You have two options for navigating to the product composites associated with a role.

To show the Products pool for a role:

Choose Go To Products Detail on a role.
or
Show the detail of a role, then show the detail of the Products pool.
