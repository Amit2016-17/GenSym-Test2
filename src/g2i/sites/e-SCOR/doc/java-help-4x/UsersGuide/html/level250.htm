Configuring Product Composite Parameters

You configure parameters for each source and delivery product that e-SCOR creates. Source and delivery products, or product composites, exist on the detail of the Products pool, which is located on the role detail.

To configure product composite parameters:

1 Do either of the following to go to the Products pool detail:
Choose Go To Products Detail from a role.
or
Show the detail of a role, then show the detail of the Products pool:
Each source and delivery product associated with the role appears in the pool.
2 Display the properties dialog for each source and delivery product to configure its parameters.
Note: If no product composites appear in the Products pool, you have not yet assigned products specifications to the role. For details, see Configuring the Products a Role Sources and Delivers.
