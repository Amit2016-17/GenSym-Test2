1 Generate order.
1.1 The Source category of the buyer generates a customer or replenishment order, which is part of the order-processing and fulfillment process, described in Order/Product Process. The Consumer role generates customer orders, and the Distributor or Manufacturer roles generate replenishment orders.
For orders that originate in a Consumer role, the Sc: Source Consumer category computes the order cost by multiplying its Order Size parameter by the Purchase Cost metric of the role's source product.
For orders that originate in a Distributor or Manufacturer role, the Source category computes the order cost by multiplying the size of the replenishment order, which it receives from the Source Planning process, by the Purchase Cost.
For information on source planning, see Source Planning Process.
1.2 The Source category and the buyer role increment their Financial Obligations metric by the order cost.
1.3 The Deliver category and the supplier role increment their Financial Bookings metric by the order cost.
2 Send shipment.
The Deliver category of the supplier sends the product shipment to the buyer, based on the order-processing and fulfillment process, described in Order/Product Process.
3 Receive shipment/Generate invoice.
3.1 The Source category of the buyer receives the product shipment, which is part of the order-processing and fulfillment process, described in Order/Product Process.
Simultaneous with the buyer receiving the shipment, the supplier's Deliver category generates an invoice, which it sends to the downstream buyer. The Source category applies the Customer Invoicing cost parameter to the Customer Accounting costs incurred metric and to the aggregate costs for the role.
3.2 When the Deliver category of the supplier sends the invoice, the Deliver category and the supplier role decrement their Financial Bookings metric by the amount of the order, and they increment their Financial Outstanding metric by the amount of the payment.
4 Receive invoice.
When the Source category of the buyer receives the invoice, it waits the amount of time indicated by the Financial Payment Terms parameter of the supplier role before sending the payment.
5 Send payment.
5.1 The Source category of the buyer sends a payment to the supplier for the order, which decrements the Financial Obligations metric and increments the Financial Payments Total metric for the Source category and the buyer role.
5.2 The Source category applies the Supply Chain Finance cost parameter to its Supply Chain Finance costs incurred metric and to the aggregate cost metrics associated with the buyer role.
6 Receive payment.
6.1 The Deliver category of the supplier receives the payment, which decrements the Financial Outstanding metric and increments the Financial Collections Total metric for the Deliver category and the supplier role.
6.2 The Deliver category applies the Customer Collections cost parameter to its Customer Accounting costs incurred metric and to the aggregate costs for the role.
