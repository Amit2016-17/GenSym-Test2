Assigning a Product Specification to a Role Interactively

You can assign a product specification to a role interactively by choosing either:

The role to which a product specification is assigned.
The product specification to which a role is assigned.

To assign a product specification to a role interactively:

1 For each product specification in the product hierarchy, assign the product specification to a role, as follows:
Choose the Choose Role menu choice on a product specification, then choose the Select menu choice on the associated role.
or
Choose the Choose Product menu choice on a role, then choose the Select menu choice on the associated product specification.
A magenta arrow appears next to the selected role.
2 Choose Hide Indicator on the indicator arrow or press the Ctrl key and click the mouse button to remove it.

You can also choose Clear Indicators from the Simulation menu to clear all indicators or click the equivalent toolbar button:

Tip: The Clear Indicators menu choice and toolbar button are only available when the Scenario is active. To activate the Scenario tool, choose Activate from the Simulation menu or click the equivalent toolbar button:
