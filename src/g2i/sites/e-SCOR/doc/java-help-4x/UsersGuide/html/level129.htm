Configuring the Time Period for Computing Time-Weighted Statistics

e-SCOR computes a variety of time-weighted statistics for various Level 2 category and product composite metrics, such as averages and standard deviations. You can view these statistics in the Metrics toolbar on the Inventory Statistics and on the Orders and Shipments Statistics tabs. You can also add these and other statistics to the appropriate Level 2 output reports in Excel.

By default, e-SCOR computes time-weighted statistics, based on a yearly time period. This means, for example, that the average represents the average value over a year-long period. If you are running your simulation for a specific time period, such as three months, you might want to compute time-weighted statistics, based on the duration of the simulation. To do this, you configure the Statistics Metrics Period parameter for the role.

To configure the time period for computing time-weighted statistics:

Display the properties dialog for a role, click the General tab, and configure the Statistical Metrics Period for the role to be the time period over which to compute time-weighted statistics for Level 2 metrics.
