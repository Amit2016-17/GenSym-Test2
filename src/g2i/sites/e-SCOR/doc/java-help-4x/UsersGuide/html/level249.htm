Default Source and Delivery Products for Each Role

This table describes the source and delivery products that e-SCOR creates when you assign a product specification to a role:

For this type of role...
e-SCOR creates...

Base Manufacturer

Delivery products for each product specification you assign to the role.

If the product specification you assign has components, the role ignores the components.

Distributor

If the product specification you assign is a finished product with components, a delivery product for the finished product and source products for each component.

If the product specification you assign is a base component, the role creates a product composite that is both a source and delivery product.

Manufacturer

Delivery products for each product specification you assign to the role, and source products for each component. The product specification you assign must be a finished product with components.

Consumer

Source products for each product specification you assign.
