Topic - Using Alternative Planning Strategies

Rules for Configuring the Planning Strategy of a Role

You must follow these rules when adding multiple Source, Make, and/or Deliver categories to the detail of any role:

For this Level 1
role configuration...
Configure the planning
strategy as follows...

Manufacturer role

The planning strategy for the Make and Deliver category must be the same, but the planning strategy for the Source category can be different.

Distributor role that delivers its inputs

The planning strategy for the Source and Deliver categories must be the same.

Distributor role that assembles components into kits

The planning strategy for the Source and Deliver categories can be different.
