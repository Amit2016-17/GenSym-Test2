Deliver Category Metrics

Group/Parameter
Tab
Description

General

Role Label

Delivery

The label associated with the role.

See Configuring General Parameters for Roles.

General

Sequence Number

Delivery

The Sequence Number of the role.

See Configuring General Parameters for Roles.

Metrics

Contracts Established

Delivery

The total number of contracts for delivery products the Deliver category has established with buyers since the start of the simulation. When no contracts are in effect, Contracts Established is 1, which means the supplier delivers products to a single buyer.

Metrics

Orders Received

Delivery

The total number of valid customer orders the Deliver category has received from buyers since the start of the simulation.

Metrics

Product Shipments
Sent

Delivery

The total number of product shipments the Deliver category has delivered to buyers since the start of the simulation.

Metrics

Mfg Ready to
Ship Time (Days)

Delivery

The sum of the Pick Material Time and Pack Material Time parameters of the Deliver category, in days.

For details, see Configuring the Deliver Category.

Metrics

Fill Rates (%)

Delivery

(D1) The percentage of product shipments the Deliver category has delivered within 24 hours of receiving an order.

Metrics

Order Entry to
Ship Time (Days)

Delivery

The average time, in days, from when the Deliver category enters a customer order to when the product shipment is ready to deliver.

General

Financial Period

Financial

The time period for computing financial metrics, obtained from the role.

General

Financial Payment
Terms

Financial

The time period for making payments from invoices, obtained from the role.

Incoming

Financial Bookings

Financial

Supplier bookings for customer orders that buyers have placed with the Deliver category but that the Deliver category has not yet invoiced. This metric includes orders for delivery products assigned to the category, using the Product Selection parameters.

Incoming

Financial Outstanding

Financial

The amount of money outstanding for customer orders that buyers have placed with the Deliver category but for which the Deliver category has not yet been paid. This metric includes orders for delivery products assigned to the category, using the Product Selection parameters.

Incoming

Financial Collections
Period

Financial

The total amount of money for product shipments the Deliver category has received from buyers during the previous collection period. This metric includes product shipments for delivery products assigned to the category, using the Product Selection parameters.

Incoming

Financial Collections
Total

Financial

The total amount of money for product shipments the Deliver category has received from buyers since the start of the simulation. This metric includes product shipments for delivery products assigned to the category, using the Product Selection parameters.

Costs Incurred

Order Entry

Cost

The total cost that the Deliver category has incurred for entering customer orders since the start of the simulation.

Costs Incurred

Order Fulfillment

Cost

The total cost that the Deliver category has incurred for filling customer orders since the start of the simulation.

Costs Incurred

Distribution

Cost

The total cost that the Deliver category has incurred for distributing product shipments to buyers since the start of the simulation.

Costs Incurred

Transportation

Cost

The total cost that the Deliver category has incurred for transporting product shipments since the start of the simulation.

Costs Incurred

Customer Accounting

Cost

The total cost that the Deliver category has incurred for customer accounting, which includes Customer Invoicing and Customer Collections, since the start of the simulation.

For details, see Configuring the Deliver Category.
