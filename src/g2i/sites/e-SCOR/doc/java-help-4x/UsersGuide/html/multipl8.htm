Configuring the Supplier Preference for the Delivery Product

You can configure the Award Criteria parameters of the buyer to choose suppliers, based on the Supplier Preference of the supplier's delivery product.

The following figure shows the Product tab of the properties dialog for a delivery product of a supplier role:

To configure the supplier preference for the delivery product:

1 Show the detail of the Products pool for each supplier role.
2 For each supplier, display the properties dialog for the delivery product.
3 Configure the Supplier Preference parameter for each delivery product to rank the suppliers by preference.

You can now configure the Award Criteria parameters, based on Supplier Preference. For details, see Configuring the Award Criteria for Choosing Suppliers.
