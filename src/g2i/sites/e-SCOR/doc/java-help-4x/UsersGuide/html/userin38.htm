Shrink Wrapping Workspaces

When you move items on a workspace beyond the visible borders, the borders adjust to fit the items. When you move items on a workspace such that the workspace contains extra space at its borders, you can adjust the borders by shrink wrapping the workspace. Shrink wrapping a workspace also adjusts the window size.

You can shrink wrap individual workspaces or all workspaces in the hierarchy, including all details in the model.

To shrink wrap an individual workspace:

Select a workspace, then choose Workspace > Shrink Wrap or click the equivalent toolbar button, located at the bottom of the window:

To shrink wrap all workspaces in the hierarchy:

Choose Format Workspace Hierarchy on a workspace to shrink wrap the current workspace and all workspaces below it in the hierarchy.
