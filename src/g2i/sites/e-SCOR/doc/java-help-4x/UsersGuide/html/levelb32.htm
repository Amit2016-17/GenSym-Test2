Verifying the Assignment of Resources to Roles

To verify the configuration of the resources, you can show the role associated with the resource, and vice versa.

To show the role associated with a resource:

Click a resource and choose Show Roles.

A magenta arrow appears next to the role associated with that resource.

To show the manufacturing resources associated with a role:

Click a role and choose Show Plants.

A magenta arrow appears next to the resource associated with that role.
