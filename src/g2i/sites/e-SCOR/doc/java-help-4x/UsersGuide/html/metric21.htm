Viewing Metrics for the Deliver Categories

The properties dialogs for the Deliver categories are the same except for this metric on the Delivery tab: The D2 and D3 categories do not have the Fill Rates (%) metric.

The Order, Fulfillment, and Transportation tabs have no computed metrics.
