Mb: Make Base
Manufacturer

Base Manufacturer

You configure the order type, which can be stock, make-to-order, or engineer-to-order, timing parameters related to manufacturing a batch of products, and the engineering time and costs associated with making engineer-to-order products.

M1: Make-to-Stock

Manufacturer

You configure whether to make complete or partial batches, the criteria for determining which orders to build first and build size cutoffs, timing parameters related to manufacturing a batch of stock products, and the costs associated with handling components.

M2: Make-to-Order

Manufacturer

You configure whether to make complete or partial batches, timing parameters related to manufacturing a batch of make-to-order products, and the costs associated with handling components.

M3: Engineer-to-Order

Manufacturer

You configure timing parameters related to manufacturing engineer-to-order products, the engineering time and cost associated with making engineer-to-order products, and the costs associated with handling components
