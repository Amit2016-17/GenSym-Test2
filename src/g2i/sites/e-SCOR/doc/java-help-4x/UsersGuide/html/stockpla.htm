Using Stock Planning Strategies

By default, each role's detail includes an S1: Source Stocked Product, M1: Make-to-Stock, and D1: Deliver Stocked Product category, as appropriate for each role. This means that, by default, each role uses a stock planning strategy to source, make, and deliver its products.

When using a stock planning strategy, the role:

Places source and delivery products in inventory.
Delivers products to any buyer that places an order.

To use a stock planning strategy, you:

Configure the stock planning strategy by configuring the role's Plan category. The Plan category you configure depends on the role.
Configure source and delivery product parameters according to the stock planning strategy you use:
- Stock/Replenishment - Creates customer and build orders for as many source and delivery products, respectively, as are required to maintain desired inventory levels, given current orders for source and delivery products.
- Stock/Forecast - Creates customer and build orders for as many source and delivery products, respectively, as are required to meet forecasts, based on buyer contracts.
Analyze the performance of the stock planning strategy by viewing metrics.
