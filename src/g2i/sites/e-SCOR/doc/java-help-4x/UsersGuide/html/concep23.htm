Topic - e-SCOR Concepts and Terminology

Products

Each Level 1 role sources, makes, and/or delivers products, based on a product specification, which defines the product name or SKU and the number of units.

To model the products that your supply chain sources, makes, and delivers, you create:

A product hierarchy, which is a hierarchical representation of the relationship between each product specification in the supply chain.
Product composites, which determine the products each role sources and/or delivers.

You create the product hierarchy on the detail of a product structure, a container in which you create the product hierarchy.
