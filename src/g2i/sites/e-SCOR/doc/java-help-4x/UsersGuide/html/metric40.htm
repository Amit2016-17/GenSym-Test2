Group/Metric
Description

Metrics

Purchase Cost

The weighted average of the price a buyer pays for its source products, which is based on the Net Selling Price parameter of the upstream supplier's delivery product and the number of units purchased.

Note: For a Base Manufacturer, Purchase Cost is a parameter that you configure to determine the cost of raw materials for the role.

Metrics

Quantity Ordered

The total number of source products a buyer has ordered from its suppliers since the start of the simulation.

Metrics

Quantity Received

The total number of source products a buyer has received from its suppliers since the start of the simulation.

Metrics

Received Average
Fulfillment Time (Days)

The average amount of time from when a buyer orders source products to when the buyer receives product shipments, in days.

Metrics

Incoming Amount

The total number of source products a buyer has received from suppliers but has not yet put into inventory. The Incoming Amount increments during the Receive and Putaway Time parameter of the Source category and decrements when the source products go into inventory.

Note: This metric is not relevant for the source product of a Consumer role, because Consumer roles do not keep an inventory of source products.
