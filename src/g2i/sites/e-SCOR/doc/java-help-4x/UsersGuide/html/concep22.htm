Topic - e-SCOR Concepts and Terminology

Consumer Role

The Consumer role represents the end of the supply chain and must be the furthest customer role in the model.

The Consumer role includes one or more Level 2 SCOR categories from these management processes and these specific Level 2 SCOR categories:

ES: Enable Source - Determines how the role choose its suppliers and manages contracts with suppliers.
Sc: Source Consumer - Sources stock, make-to-order, and/or engineer-to-order products from upstream suppliers.
