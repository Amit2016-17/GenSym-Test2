Creating the Product Hierarchy

e-SCOR allows you to model and simulate a supply chain for a particular product hierarchy. To model the product hierarchy, you create one or more top-level consumer products. Each consumer product consists of one or more components and/or finished products, and each finished product consists of one or more components or finished products, and so on, until there are only components.

To create the product hierarchy, you:

Determine the level of detail in the product hierarchy,
Then, depending on the level of detail, you either:
- Create a product hierarchy with a single product specification, or
- Create a product hierarchy with components.
