Topic - What Happens When the Simulation Runs

Contracts Process

When contracts are in effect, the process of determining qualified suppliers is similar to the process that occurs when the model initialized, described in the Role Verification Process, with these exceptions:

When contacts are in effect, source planning initiates the process that sends purchase requests for source products; whereas, model initialization initiates the process that determines qualified suppliers at startup.
When contracts are in effect, purchase responses and purchase awards have a unique identification number; whereas, these objects have an identification number of 0 when the model determines qualified suppliers at startup.

1 Send purchase request.
After waiting the amount of time indicated by the Initial Contract Delay parameter of the Source category, each Contract Duration parameter, the buyer's ES: Enable Source category sends a purchase request to each upstream supplier in the model. The buyer performs this step for each of its source products.
The ES category waits the amount of time indicated by the Contract Response Time parameter of the Source category, then issues purchase requests to each qualified supplier.
2 Send purchase response.
If an upstream supplier can deliver the requested product, the ED: Enable Delivery category of each qualified supplier responds by sending a purchase response back to the buyer. The ES category of the buyer stores the purchase response in its Responses pool on the role detail.
3 Send purchase award.
The ES category increments its Contracts Established metric by one.
The ES category of the buyer looks at the Award Criteria parameters, the Number of Suppliers parameter, the Split Order parameter, and the Proportion parameters of the Source category to determine the size of the award for each selected supplier, based on estimated amounts. The ES category places the purchase award in its Buyer Awards pool on the role detail. It then creates a copy of each purchase award and sends them to each supplier that sent a purchase response.
The Deliver category increments it Contracts Established metric by one.
