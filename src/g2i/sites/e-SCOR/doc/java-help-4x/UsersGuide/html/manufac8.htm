Topic - Modeling a Manufacturing Process

What Happens When the Simulation Runs

Similar to a Distributor role, a Manufacturer role orders source products from its upstream suppliers and delivers finished products to its downstream buyers. The similarities and differences are as follows. A Manufacturer role:

Determines its available suppliers when the model initializes, just like a Distributor role.
Must source components, whereas a Distributor role can source components or finished products.
Must deliver finished products, whereas a Distributor role can deliver components or finished products.
Uses source planning to determine when to order source products, just like a Distributor role.
Uses make planning to determine when to make finished products, just like a Base Manufacturer role.
Computes incoming and outgoing financial metrics on a regular financial cycle, just like a Distributor role.
