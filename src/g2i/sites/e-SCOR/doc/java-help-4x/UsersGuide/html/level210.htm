ES: Enable Source

Distributor
Manufacturer

You do not configure any parameters for the ES category.

Sc: Source Consumer

Consumer

You configure the size and frequency of orders for consumer products, the criteria the role uses to choose suppliers and determine order size when contracts are in effect, and the costs associated with sourcing products. The Sc category can source stock, make-to-order, or engineer-to-order products.

S1: Source
Stocked Product

Distributor
Manufacturer

You configure the time it takes to receive stock products, the criteria the role uses to choose suppliers and determine order size when contracts are in effect, and the costs associated with sourcing stock products.
