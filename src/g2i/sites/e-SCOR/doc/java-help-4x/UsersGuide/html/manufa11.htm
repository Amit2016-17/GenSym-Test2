Topic - Modeling a Manufacturing Process

Level 2 Metrics to Analyze

To analyze the performance of a manufacturing process at Level 2, it is helpful to think about the manufacturing process in terms of the upstream source subprocess, the make subprocess, and the downstream delivery subprocess. Each of these subprocesses has metrics you can analyze.

Upstream source subprocess
ES: Enable Source category:
- Orders Sent and Product Shipments Received
- Supplier On-Time Performance (%)
Source category:
- Product Shipments Received
- Product Shipments Lead Time Delta (Days)
- Financial Period and Financial Payment Terms
- Financial Obligations, Financial Payments Period, and Financial Payments Total
- Product Acquisition, Create Customer Order and Supply Chain Finance costs
P2: Plan Source category:
- Number of Planning Periods
Source products:
- Inventory Level, Potential Inventory Level, and Inventory Status
- Purchase Cost
- Quantity Ordered and Quantity Received
- Received Average Fulfillment Time (Days)
- Incoming Amount
- On Order
Make subprocess
Make categories:
- Build Orders Started and Build Orders Completed
- Make Cycle Time (Days)
- Order Entry to Mfg Time of the M3: Engineer-to-Order category
- Production Material Handling cost
- ECO cost of the M3: Engineer-to-Order category
P3: Plan Make category:
- Number of Planning Periods
Delivery products:
- Work in Progress
- Total Products Accepted and Total Products Rejected
Downstream delivery subprocess
ED: Enable Delivery category:
- Orders Received and Product Shipments Sent
- Delivery Performance (%)
- Perfect Order Fulfillment (%)
Deliver categories:
- Orders Received and Product Shipments Sent
- Mfg Ready to Ship Time (Days)
- Fill Rates (%)
- Order Entry to Ship Time (Days)
- Financial Period and Financial Payment Terms
- Financial Bookings, Financial Outstanding, Financial Collections Period, and Financial Collections Total
- Order Entry, Order Fulfillment, Distribution, Transportation, and Customer Accounting costs
Delivery products:
- Inventory Level, Potential Inventory Level, and Inventory Status
- Order Fulfillment Lead Time (Days)
- Quantity to Deliver and Quantity Shipped
- In Transit Amount
- Awaiting Order Total and On Order
