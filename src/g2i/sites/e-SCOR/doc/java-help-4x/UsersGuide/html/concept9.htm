SCOR Management Processes

A SCOR model consists of four distinct management processes:

Plan - Plan supply and demand cycles.
Source - Obtain materials from suppliers for manufacture, distribution, or consumption.
Make - Produce manufactured products.
Deliver - Distribute ordered products, manage orders, and ship products.
