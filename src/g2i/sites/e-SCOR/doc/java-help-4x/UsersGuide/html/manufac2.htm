Configuring a Manufacturer Role

Your supply chain might include a manufacturing process, which sources components from upstream suppliers, and makes and delivers finished products. Unlike a Distributor role, which can source components or finished products, a Manufacturer role must source components and it must make and deliver finished products.

The following figure shows a model in which the Manufacturer role manufactures components into finished products. The Manufacturer role sources components from the Base Manufacturer role and manufactures finished computers. When you explicitly assign the finished product to the role, e-SCOR creates product composites for both the finished product and the component. The finished product is configured to be a delivery product, and the components are configured to be source products.

To configure a Manufacturer role:

1 Create a product hierarchy that contains a finished product with components.
For details, see Creating a Product Hierarchy with Components.
2 Assign to the Manufacturer role the product specification that represents the finished product.
When you assign the finished product to the role, e-SCOR automatically assigns the components to the role, as well.
For details, see Configuring the Products a Role Sources and Delivers.
3 Configure the Completion Preference of the delivery product to be Complete Only.
Note: The Completion Preference for a Manufacturer role should always be Complete Only, never Complete or Assembled, which are the options for a Distributor role.
