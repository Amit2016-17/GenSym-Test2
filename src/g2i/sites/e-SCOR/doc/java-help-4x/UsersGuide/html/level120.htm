Assigning a Product Specification to a Role Through the Role Dialog

You can assign a product specification to a role by choosing the product specification from a tree representation of the product hierarchy on the role dialog.

To assign a product specification to a role through the role dialog:

1 Display the properties dialog for a role and click the Products tab.
A tree representation of the product hierarchy appears. The tree is expanded, by default. You can expand and collapse the tree by clicking the plus and minus signs next to each product structure.
2 Click a product specification in the tree to assign it to the role.
When you assign a delivery product to a Manufacturer role and click Apply, both the source and delivery products are selected.
