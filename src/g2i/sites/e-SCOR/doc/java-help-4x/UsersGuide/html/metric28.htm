Metrics

Orders Received

Delivery

The total number of customer orders the ED category has received from buyers since the start of the simulation. This metric includes orders for all delivery products associated with the role.

Metrics

Product Shipments
Sent

Delivery

The total number of product shipments the ED category has sent to buyers since the start of the simulation. This metric includes product shipments for all delivery products associated with the role.

Metrics

Delivery
Performance (%)

Delivery

The percentage of product shipments the ED category has delivered on or before the customer request date. The ED category computes this date from the Desired Turnaround parameter of the source product of the downstream buyer role. This metric includes product shipments for all delivery products associated with the role.

Metrics

Perfect Order
Fulfillment (%)

Delivery

The percentage of product shipments the ED category has delivered on or before the customer request date and that are for the requested quantities. The ED category computes this date from the Desired Turnaround parameter of the source product of the downstream buyer role. This metric includes product shipments for all delivery products associated with the role.
