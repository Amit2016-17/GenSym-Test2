Scaling Workspaces

You can scale a workspace to fit the current window, or zoom a workspace in, out, or to a specific scale.

To scale a workspace:

Adjust the size of the workspace window to the desired size; the workspace scales to fit the window automatically.
or
Choose View > Zoom In or Zoom Out, enter Ctrl + = to zoom in or Ctrl + - (minus) to zoom out, or click the equivalent toolbar buttons:
or
Choose View > Zoom or enter Ctrl + M, then choose or enter a zoom scale, or enter a specific zoom scale in the zoom scale on the toolbar:
