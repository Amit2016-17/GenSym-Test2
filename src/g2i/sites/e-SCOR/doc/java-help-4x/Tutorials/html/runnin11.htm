Viewing Delivery Product Metrics: Base Manufacturer Role

The delivery product of a supplier role computes:

Current inventory level for delivery products and potential inventory, based on available source products.
Current inventory status for delivery products, based on whether the current inventory is below the Replenishment Threshold (below-threshold), between the Replenishment Threshold and the Maximum Threshold (within-threshold), or above the Maximum Threshold (above-threshold).
Total number of components the manufacturer role has taken from inventory and is currently using to make finished products and are, thus, in progress (Work in Progress).
Total number of delivery products accepted and rejected, which is based on the Yield parameter of the role's delivery product.
The average amount of time from when the supplier role receives an order for the delivery product to when the buyer actually receives the product shipment (Order Fulfillment Lead Time).
Total number of orders to deliver and total number of products shipped for the delivery product since the start of the simulation.
Total number of delivery products the supplier has taken from inventory but has not yet shipped and are, thus, in transit (In Transit Amount).
Current number of delivery products waiting to be ordered (Amount to Order).
Current number of delivery products waiting to be delivered (Awaiting Order Total).
The number of delivery products on order but the supplier has not yet entered or delivered (On Order).

You can view metrics related to inventory and metrics related to orders and shipments through the dialogs and through the Metrics toolbar. The Metrics toolbar shows key metrics, as well as statistics related to these metrics.

To view delivery product metrics for the Base Manufacturer role:

1 While the simulation is paused, click the Product Inventories tab in the Metrics toolbar to view key product inventory metrics.
2 Click the Inventory Statistics tab to view averages and standard deviations for product inventories.
3 Click the Orders and Shipments tab to view key metrics related to orders and shipments.
4 Click the Orders and Shipments Statistics tab to view averages and standard deviations for orders and shipments.
5 Now, display the properties dialog for the delivery product in the Products pool on the Base Manufacturer role detail and view these metrics in one location:
