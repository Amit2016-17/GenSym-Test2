Configuring Product Composite Parameters

You configure different parameters for product composites depending on whether the product composite is a source product or a delivery product. The dialog tab pages determine which parameters you need to configure, as follows:

Tab
Description

Product

Order type and preferences for both source and delivery products.

Inventory

Initial and maximum inventory for both source and delivery products of Base Manufacturer, Distributor, and Manufacturer roles; not relevant for Consumer roles.

Source

Order parameters, contract and forecast amount, multipliers for Source category timing parameters for source products of buyer roles only.

Manufacture

Batch and yield parameters, and multipliers for Make category timing parameters for delivery products of manufacturer roles only.

Delivery

Selling price, published lead time, and multipliers for Deliver category timing parameters for delivery products of supplier roles only.

Planning

Stock/replenishment and push planning parameters for both source and delivery products of Base Manufacturer, Distributor, and Manufacturer roles; not relevant for Consumer roles.

You can configure multiple product composites at the same time through an Excel report, rather than using dialogs, which you will do later in this tutorial.

To configure product composite parameters:

1 Display the Products pool detail for the Base Manufacturer role.
For details, see Viewing Product Composites.
2 Display the properties dialog for the delivery product of the Base Manufacturer role, then click the Product, Inventory, Manufacture, Delivery, and Planning tabs to familiarize yourself with each of the tab pages.
You do not configure parameters on the Source tab for delivery products.
3 Display the properties dialog for the source product of the Consumer role, then click the Product, Inventory, and Source tabs.

You do not configure parameters on the Manufacture, Delivery, or Planning tabs for the source product of a Consumer role.

Next, you will configure the required parameters for each product composite.
