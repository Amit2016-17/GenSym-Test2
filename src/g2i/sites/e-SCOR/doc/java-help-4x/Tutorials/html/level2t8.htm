Viewing Product Composites

When you assign a product specification to a role, e-SCOR creates different product composites for different roles, as follows:

A Base Manufacturer role has only delivery products.
A Consumer role has only source products.
A Manufacturer role has both source and delivery products, where the source products are components and the delivery products are finished products.
A Distributor role that delivers its input products has both source and delivery products, where the source and delivery products are the same.
A Distributor role that assembles components into kits has both source and delivery products, just like a Manufacturer role.

You will create and view product composites for Distributor and Manufacturer roles later in this tutorial.

You can use one of two techniques for viewing product composites for the Base Manufacturer and Consumer roles.

To view the product composites for a role:

1 Show the Products pool detail for the Base Manufacturer and Consumer roles, using each of the following techniques:
Choose the Go To Products Detail on the role.
or
Show the role detail, then choose Show Detail on the Products pool on the detail.
e-SCOR places the product composite at a random location on the detail. Typically, you need to adjust its location.
2 On each Products pool, drag the product composite to the upper-left corner of the workspace, then shrink wrap the workspace and adjust the window size.
