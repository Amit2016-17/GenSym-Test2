Configuring Financial Parameters: Simplest Model

Each role has a Financial Period that it uses for computing its financial, cost, and asset metrics. Each role also has a Financial Payment Terms that downstream buyer roles use to determine the amount of time the buyer has to make payments for goods received. Therefore, the supplier role determines the payment terms.

A Consumer role ignores its Financial Payment Terms, because it is not a supplier.

In the simplest model, configure the financial parameters so that:

The Base Manufacturer role computes financial metrics once every 30 days, the default.
The Consumer role computes financial metrics once every 30 days, the default.
The Consumer role has 30 days to make payments from invoices, based on the Financial Payment Terms of the supplier role, the default.
