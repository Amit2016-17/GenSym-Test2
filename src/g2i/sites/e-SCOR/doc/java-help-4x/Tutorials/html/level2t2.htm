Viewing Consumer Role Details

The Consumer role detail includes these process categories:

ES: Enable Source category, which is responsible for sending customer orders-orders for source products with upstream suppliers-and establishing contracts with those suppliers.
Sc: Source Consumer category, which defines the finished products the supply chain consumes, the frequency with which the role places orders for those products, and the size of those orders.

Distributor, Manufacturer, and Consumer roles all require the ES: Enable Source categories on the role detail. Customer orders that originate in a Distributor or Manufacturer role are also known as replenishment orders.

A Consumer role requires the Sc category as its Source category, whereas a Distributor and Manufacturer role uses the S1: Source Stocked Product category as the default Source category.

You can also include the S2: Source Make-to-Order Product and/or the S3: Source Engineer-to-Order Product categories on the role detail to support alternative planning strategies.

To view role details:

Choose Show Detail on the Consumer role.
