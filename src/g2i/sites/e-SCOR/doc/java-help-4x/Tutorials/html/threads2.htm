Topic - What Happens When the Simulation Runs

Role Verification Process

The Role Verification process ensures that the model can source the necessary components from suppliers to make and deliver the finished products assigned to the role. The steps are:

1 Send purchase requests to suppliers.
2 Send purchase responses to buyers in response to those purchase requests.
3 Receive and verifying responses.
4 Send purchase awards to suppliers in response to purchase responses.
5 Save purchase awards in the Awards pool.

The Role Verification process also verifies the interaction of pull and push planning modes for each role. The simplest model uses pull planning for both roles.

The process initiates when the simulation begins.

If you have configured the model incorrectly, the model does not create the desired delivery products, based on orders.

To view the Role Verification process:

1 Show the details of the Consumer and Base Manufacturer roles.
Tip: Choose Tile Vertically or Tile Horizontally from the Workspace menu to show both details simultaneously.
2 Choose Start All from the Simulation menu or click the equivalent toolbar button to reset and start the simulation running.

In the first two seconds of simulation time, e-SCOR runs the Role Verification process. First, the ES: Enable Source category of the downstream buyer role generates a purchase request for its source products. If the upstream supplier role can deliver the requested product, the ED: Enable Delivery category of the supplier role generates a purchase response for its delivery products. The ES: Enable Source category of the buyer role then generates a purchase award and sends it to the supplier that sent the purchase response. You can see these objects flowing through the model on the role details.

The buyer role stores purchase awards in its Buyer Awards pool, and the supplier role stores purchase awards in its Supplier Awards pool. Additionally, the supplier role stores purchase responses in its Responses pool. These pools are located on the role detail, similar to the Products pool.
