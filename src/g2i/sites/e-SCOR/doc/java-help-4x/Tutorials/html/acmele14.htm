Mb of Base Manufacturer Cases (Make Parameters Report)

Parameter
Description

Order Release To Mfg Min = 7
Order Release To Mfg Mode = 8
Order Release To Mfg Max = 9

Starts manufacturing batches of cases 8 hours, on average, after receiving a build order.

Mfg Min = 8
Mfg Mode = 10
Mfg Max = 12

Takes 10 hours, plus or minus 2 hours, to manufacture a batch of cases.

Move To Delivery Min = .5
Move To Delivery Mode = 1
Move To Delivery Max = 1.5

Takes 1 hour, on average, to transfer cases from where they are manufactured to where they are delivered.
