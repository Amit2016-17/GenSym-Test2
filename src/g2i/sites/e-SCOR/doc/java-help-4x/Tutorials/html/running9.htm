Viewing Deliver Category Metrics

The Deliver categories compute:

The number of contracts the role has established with downstream buyers. When no contracts are in effect, the number of contracts established is 1, which means the supplier role has a single buyer for its delivery products. When contracts are in effect, the supplier might have several buyers, in which case the number of contracts established could be a number greater than 1.
Total orders received and total product shipments sent since the start of the simulation.
The number of days it takes to pick delivery products from inventory and pack containers (Mfg Ready to Ship Time).
The percentage of orders the role has delivered within 24 hours of receiving the order (Fill Rates).
The average number of days from when the role receives an order to when the order is actually ready to ship (Order Entry to Ship Time).
Financial bookings, outstanding, and collections for individual delivery products that use the same planning strategy-stock, make-to-order, or engineer-to-order.
Total cost of entering, fulfilling, distributing, shipping, invoicing, and collecting payments for orders.
